Ikamva Sports & Performing Arts

Ikamva Sports and Perfoming Arts Initiative #Making A Change

Ikamva Sports and Perfoming Arts Initiative was established in November 2014 and its focus in providing sports coaches to schools in Samora Machel Township.
This organization is mainly focusing on Sports and Education(Educational Trip).


we are an NPO which is focused on Sports in Primary Schools.
we starting to spread by April in order to reach our target of 50 Primary Schools.
we in need of 250Coaches in cetain regions.

Background

Est. March 2015
Not For Profit Org. (153-059)
Demographic Area: Samora Machel, Philippi Township (RDP Housing area, with 2 Informal Settlements namely Cosovo & Sweet Home Farm)
4 Primary Schools and 2 High Schools

Where do we do our Work?

We have adopted a “School” called Nal’xolo Primary School in Samora Machel, Philippi.
We work from our offices in Tsoga Centre from 09:00 till 17:30pm for administrative duties
We do our practical work (Coaching/Training) at Nal’xolo Primary School from 13:00-16:30pm
We “Oversee” Community Sports Programme at Tsoga Centre from 17:00 (5 side games)

What we Do?

We provide “After-School Programmes”
We provide Physical Education Programmes(Grade R-3)
We provide Sports & Recreation Coaching Solutions (Grade 1-7) & 13-18 (Community Sports Programme)
We Coach the following Sports Codes
Netball, Cricket, Soccer, Rugby & Athletics (Boys & Girls)
We provide arts & culture programmes (Dance, Music, Acting)

We starting to spread by April in order to reach our target of 50 Primary Schools.
We in need of 250 Coaches in cetain regions (Samora Machel).
